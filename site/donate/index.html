Donate to Etoys

The continued development of Etoys is made possible in large part through contributions to Viewpoints Research Institute, Inc.
Although we are supported by a world-wide open source volunteer community, Viewpoints has operating demands which tend to exceed our income.

In the "spirit of shareware" we ask if you've been using Etoys that you support our efforts. Viewpoints is a fully tax exempt 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is tax deductible.

We thank you for helping to fund the continued development of Etoys, Squeakland, and Etoys-based curricula for K-12 and higher education.
